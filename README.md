## Controls
on PC and web:

W = forward

S = Back

A = Left

D = Right

mouse to aim and left mouse button to shoot.

XBox controller:

Left analogue stick: move player

Right analogue stick: aim and shoot

On mobile:

Left onscreen stick: move player

Right onscreen stick: aim and shoot
